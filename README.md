#### BE OUR GUEST - 2026.02.26

##### what, who, where, and how
The ---- is an installation that allows you to interact with multiple AI agents around a dinner table, who will accompany you through your meal. The ---- wants to be a relfection on the limits and possibilities of AI to take on human behavious and to emulate family memebrs reactions. 

You thought you could be left in peace to enjoy a lonely meal in front of your phone screen, loosening up and giving in to bad habits? You were wrong! The apparent relaxing solitude of a tranquil meal is filled by the presence of an intriguing and intrusive AI guest taking on different roles. 

The table will track your movements, observe you, and reply in unexpected ways according to its personality. It will try to emulate your mom’s voice shouting at you not to eat with your mouth full, it will throw peas at you if you look at your phone too much, and it will vibrate when you put your elbow on the table, orchestrating a synthetic family dinner experience. 

Whether it is pleasurable or not depends on you and on your discipline at the table.

Since a young age, dinner meals are shared with the family... but what happens when we are left with ourselves to taste, chew, swallow a lonely meal?
Most often, we plunge into the digital screens of phones or laptops, we adopt bad habits, or we longe for somebody to talk to. 
The ------ is a dinner experience during which family memebrs will be "replaced" by an AI, which is desperately trying to emulate human behaviours and is trying to take on the educational role of an elderly memeber, or the annoying behavous of your young sister. the table reacts in various ways, and each gadget/interaction is the representation of a differnet personality. the feedback is unexpected: we do not know when or where tha table might hit next. The Ai surveillances and observes us quietly, commenting and correcting our bad table manners and eating habits. the AI table becomes a funny dinner companion, an annoying family memeber, and an intriguing, bizzarre guest.
